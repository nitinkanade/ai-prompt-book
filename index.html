Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the AI Prompt Book app.

Is AI Prompt Book free?

Yes. AI Prompt Book is a free Android app. You get the full curated prompt library, one-tap copy, Photo Remix, offline favorites, and search — no subscription required.

Which AI image generators do the prompts work with?

Every prompt is tested against popular generators including Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E (ChatGPT), and Gemini. Copy a prompt in the app and paste it into whichever tool you use.

What is Photo Remix?

Photo Remix pairs a photo from your gallery with a tested style prompt, so you can recreate trending looks — 3D figurines, anime portraits, cinematic edits — with yourself as the subject.

Do the prompts work offline?

Saved favorites work offline. Preview images for your favorite prompts are stored locally on your device, so your collection is available even without a connection.

Is AI Prompt Book available on iPhone?

The app is currently Android-only and available on Google Play. You can still use all the prompts from our blog guides in any AI tool on any device.
