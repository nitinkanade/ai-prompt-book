AI Prompt Book puts a curated library of tested image prompts in your pocket. Here's how to get the most out of the app.

## 1. Install the App

Download [AI Prompt Book from Google Play]({{ site.play_store_url }}) — it's free. The app works on any modern Android device and downloads are lightweight.

## 2. Browse Curated Categories

Open the app and explore prompt categories like **Anime, Fantasy, Wallpapers, Logos, and Characters**. Every prompt in the library has been tested against real AI image generators, and each one includes a preview image so you know exactly what style to expect before you use it.

## 3. Copy a Prompt in One Tap

Found a style you love? Tap the **Copy** button on any prompt card. The full prompt text — including quality keywords and model parameters — is copied to your clipboard. Then paste it straight into your favorite generator:

- **Midjourney** (Discord or web)
- **Stable Diffusion** (any UI)
- **DALL-E / ChatGPT**
- **Gemini**

## 4. Remix With Your Own Photo

This is where AI Prompt Book shines. Use **Photo Remix** to pick a photo from your gallery and pair it with any prompt from the library. The app combines your subject with the prompt's style, so you can recreate trending looks — 3D figurines, retro portraits, cinematic edits — starring you.

1. Open any prompt you like.
2. Tap **Remix with Photo**.
3. Choose an image from your gallery.
4. Follow the guided steps to generate your remix in your AI tool of choice.

## 5. Save Favorites for Offline Use

Tap the heart icon on any prompt to save it to **Favorites**. Preview images are stored locally on your device, so your saved collection works even without an internet connection — perfect for capturing ideas on the go.

## 6. Search Smarter

Use the built-in search to find prompts by style, subject, tag, or target generator. Recent searches are remembered, so returning to a style you explored earlier takes one tap.
