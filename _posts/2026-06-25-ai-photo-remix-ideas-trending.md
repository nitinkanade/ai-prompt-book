Photo remixing — feeding your own photo into an AI model along with a style prompt — is the biggest trend in AI art right now. Instead of generating random strangers, you become the subject. Here are ten remix styles worth trying, with the prompt idea behind each.

## 1. 3D Collectible Figurine

The viral classic. Try this remix idea:
> `a 1/7 scale collectible figurine of the person in this photo, displayed on a desk, acrylic base, box packaging visible in the background, studio lighting`

## 2. Retro 90s Bollywood / Hollywood Portrait

Try this remix idea:
> `Transform this photo into a 1990s film poster portrait — grainy texture, dramatic studio backdrop, vintage color grading, retro typography`

## 3. Anime Character

Try this remix idea:
> `Redraw the person in this photo as an anime character, cel-shaded, expressive eyes, detailed hair, keeping the same pose and outfit`

## 4. Renaissance Oil Painting

Try this remix idea:
> `A classical Renaissance oil painting of the person in this photo, chiaroscuro lighting, ornate gold frame, museum quality`

## 5. Cinematic Movie Poster

Try this remix idea:
> `Turn this photo into an epic action movie poster — dramatic rim lighting, lens flare, title typography, moody teal-and-orange grade`

## 6. Pixar-Style 3D Character

Try this remix idea:
> `Recreate the person as a Pixar-style 3D animated character, soft global illumination, oversized expressive eyes, friendly proportions`

## 7. Cyberpunk Neon Portrait

Try this remix idea:
> `The person in this photo as a cyberpunk character — neon rim lighting, holographic HUD elements, rain-slicked city bokeh background`

## 8. LEGO / Toy Brick Minifigure

Try this remix idea:
> `Convert the person into a toy brick minifigure inside its retail box, product photography, bright studio lighting`

## 9. Polaroid With a Celebrity-Style Caption

Try this remix idea:
> `A vintage instant photo of the person, flash photography look, white frame, handwritten caption at the bottom, slightly faded colors`

## 10. Fantasy RPG Character Card

Try this remix idea:
> `The person as a fantasy RPG character — ornate armor, glowing runes, painted card-game art style, stat-card border framing`

## How to Run These Remixes

Multimodal tools like **Gemini** and **ChatGPT** accept an uploaded photo plus a text prompt directly. For the best results:

- Use a well-lit, front-facing photo with a clean background.
- Keep the identity instruction explicit: "keep the same face and features."
- Change one style element at a time if the result drifts.

The **AI Prompt Book** Android app makes this workflow one tap: browse the remix category, pick a style you like from the preview images, hit **Remix with Photo**, and choose a photo from your gallery. The app pairs your photo with the tested prompt so you skip the trial-and-error.
