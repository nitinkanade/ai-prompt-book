_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how the **AI Prompt Book** Android app and this website (together, the "Service"), operated by ni18.in, handle your information.

## Information We Collect

- **App usage data.** The app may collect anonymous usage analytics (such as screens viewed and features used) to help us improve the Service. This data does not personally identify you.
- **Photos you select.** The Photo Remix feature accesses only the photos you explicitly choose from your gallery. Photos are processed on your device and passed to the third-party AI tool you select. We do not upload, store, or retain your photos on our servers.
- **Favorites and preferences.** Your saved prompts and settings are stored locally on your device.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, or precise location. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

The app links to third-party AI tools (such as Midjourney, Gemini, and ChatGPT) and to the Google Play Store. Content you submit to those services is governed by their own privacy policies. The Service may use Google services (such as Play Services and analytics) which are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
